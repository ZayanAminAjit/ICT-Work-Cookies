How to make homemade cookies!

Ingredients:

1 tablespoon of butter
1 tablespoon of granulated sugar
1 tablespoon of brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract
A pinch of salt
3 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon of chocolate chips

Instructions:

In a microwave-safe mug or small bowl, melt the butter in the microwave for about 20-30 seconds until it's melted but not boiling.
Stir in the granulated sugar, brown sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined.
Add the flour and mix until a dough forms.
Fold in the chocolate chips.
Flatten the dough slightly in the mug or bowl.
Microwave on high for about 40-60 seconds, depending on the wattage of your microwave. Keep an eye on it to avoid overcooking.
Let it cool for a minute or two before enjoying your quick microwave cookie!
